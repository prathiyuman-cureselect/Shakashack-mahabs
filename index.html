About Us

Welcome to Our Beach Shack

Nestled right by the shore, our beach shack is more than just a place to grab a bite. It’s a vibrant hub where culinary passion meets coastal bliss. Here, we serve top-notch dishes prepared from scratch with love, using only the freshest catch of the day and locally sourced ingredients. Each meal is a testament to our commitment to fine dining, re-imagined in a relaxed, beachy atmosphere.

Whether you’re looking for a cozy spot to work, a venue for an intimate gathering, or a lively night out, our shack is the perfect backdrop. Picture this: a sunset-soaked evening filled with laughter, karaoke, and beer pong, all while the sound of waves serenades your groovy beats that keep the vibe alive.

Join us in our little slice of paradise, where every moment is designed to welcome, provide, and fulfill your every desire. Come for the food, stay for the fun, and leave with memories that will last a lifetime!

Highlights

Embrace the beach lifestyle with our diverse facilities - beachfront dining, ocean sports, wellness classes and groovy tunes playing all day long. Relax in style and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of our seaside retreat.

Cuisine

Savour our delectable cuisine crafted from locally sourced ingredients, offering a fusion of flavours that tantalise the taste buds. Our chefs create culinary masterpieces that reflect the essence of coastal dining, ensuring a memorable gastronomic journey for every guest.

Adventure Awaits

Embark on thrilling adventures with our array of activities, from surfing the waves to exploring the underwater world through scuba diving. Unwind with kayaking excursions, find inner peace with yoga sessions, or boost your fitness with our High-Intensity Interval Training courses. There’s something for every enthusiast at Shaka Shack!

Events

Host your special events at Shaka Shack and create unforgettable memories against the picturesque backdrop of Mahaballipuram’s coastline. Whether it’s a birthday bash, anniversary celebrations, corporate day out, or a private celebration, our team ensures seamless event planning and execution in a truly mesmerising setting.

Entertainment

By day, you can enjoy the laid-back vibes, with the sound of waves mingling with groovy beats that set the perfect seaside tone. As the sun sets, Shaka Shack comes alive with fun-filled evenings featuring karaoke, beer pong, and sundowner sessions that create a cozy beach club vibe. It’s the ideal spot to socialise, celebrate, and make memories.
